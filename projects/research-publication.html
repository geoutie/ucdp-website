About This Project

Research & Publication

Effective development work is built on evidence. UCDP's Research and Publication programme ensures that our interventions — and our advocacy — are grounded in rigorous, context-specific knowledge about the communities we serve.

We conduct and commission research on issues affecting vulnerable children, street youth, community health, and social welfare in Kisumu and the wider region. Our findings are documented, published, and shared with partners, government bodies, and the wider development community to inform better policy and practice.

Through our publications, we also share lessons learned from our own programmes — contributing to the growing body of knowledge on community development in Kenya and East Africa. Our research gives a voice to data that would otherwise remain hidden, and turns lived experience into actionable knowledge.

How It Works

Our Approach

Research Design

We identify key questions and gaps in knowledge affecting our communities and design appropriate research methodologies.

Data Collection

Research is conducted in the field — through interviews, surveys, focus groups, and direct observation within our communities.

Analysis & Findings

Data is analysed carefully and translated into clear, actionable findings that reflect the reality on the ground.

Publication & Dissemination

Findings are published and shared with partners, government bodies, and the wider development community to inform policy and practice.
