About This Project

Western Kenya Peace Initiative (WKPI)

Peace is the foundation on which all development is built. Without it, communities cannot thrive, children cannot learn, and families cannot flourish. UCDP is a committed partner of the Western Kenya Peace Initiative (WKPI) — a powerful coalition of 12 organisations working together to promote peace, reduce conflict, and build social cohesion across the western parts of Kenya.

The WKPI brings together civil society organisations, community groups, and development actors who share a common commitment to peaceful coexistence in the region. Together, the coalition addresses the root causes of conflict — including inter-community tensions, resource-based disputes, and ethnic divisions — through dialogue, mediation, community engagement, and peace education.

As a WKPI partner, UCDP contributes its community presence, networks, and expertise in working with vulnerable populations — particularly street youth and children — to the coalition's broader peace-building work. We believe that children and youth who are supported, educated, and empowered are less likely to become victims or perpetrators of conflict.

How It Works

Our Approach

Coalition Meetings

WKPI partners meet regularly to coordinate peace-building activities, share intelligence on conflict hotspots, and plan joint interventions.

Community Dialogue

The coalition facilitates community dialogues, bringing together groups in conflict to find common ground and peaceful resolution.

Peace Education

Partners conduct peace education sessions in schools, communities, and youth groups to build a culture of non-violence and dialogue.

UCDP's Contribution

UCDP contributes its community networks, expertise in youth and child welfare, and ground-level presence to the coalition's peace-building work.
