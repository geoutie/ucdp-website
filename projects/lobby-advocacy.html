Amplifying the voices of vulnerable children and communities — influencing policy, challenging injustice, and driving systemic change in Kisumu and beyond.

About This Project

Lobby & Advocacy

Direct service delivery is vital — but lasting change requires more than that. UCDP's Lobby and Advocacy work ensures that the voices and needs of the vulnerable children and communities we serve are heard at the highest levels — by policymakers, government bodies, and decision-makers who have the power to create systemic change.

We advocate for policies and practices that protect children's rights, improve access to education and healthcare, address gender inequality, and promote the welfare of street children and vulnerable youth in Kisumu County and beyond.

Our advocacy is grounded in the real experiences of the communities we work with — making it credible, evidence-based, and impossible to ignore. We engage with local government, participate in public forums, and collaborate with partner organisations to push for meaningful reform.

How It Works

Our Approach

Community Engagement

We listen to and document the experiences of the communities we serve — building an evidence base for our advocacy.

Policy Engagement

We engage with local government, county officials, and relevant authorities to push for policies that protect children and vulnerable populations.

Public Forums

We participate in and facilitate public forums, community barazas, and stakeholder meetings to raise awareness and build coalitions.

Partnerships

We collaborate with other NGOs, civil society organisations, and government bodies to amplify our advocacy impact.
