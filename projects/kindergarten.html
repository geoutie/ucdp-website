Kindergarten Programme

A completely free kindergarten for vulnerable children — including free daily lunch — giving every child the best possible start in life.

About This Project

Kindergarten Programme

The earliest years of a child's life are the most critical for development. Yet for vulnerable families in Kisumu, quality early childhood education is out of reach. UCDP's Kindergarten Programme removes every barrier — offering a fully free, nurturing, and stimulating learning environment for young children in the community.

Attendance is completely free for all children — every child enrolled comes from a vulnerable background in the community. There are no fees, no hidden costs. In addition, every child receives a free lunch during the school day, ensuring they are nourished and ready to learn.

Our kindergarten provides a safe, joyful space where children develop literacy, numeracy, social skills, and confidence — preparing them for a smooth transition into primary school.

How It Works

Our Approach

Enrolment

Vulnerable children from the community are enrolled completely free of charge — no fees, no conditions.

Daily Learning

Children participate in a structured daily programme covering literacy, numeracy, creative play, and social development.

Free Daily Lunch

Every child receives a nutritious free lunch during the school day, supporting their health and ability to learn.

School Readiness

By the time they graduate from kindergarten, children are confident, curious, and ready for primary school.
