Data Protection

How UCDP collects, uses, and protects personal data — including the use of photographs.

Our Commitment to Data Protection

Uhuru Community Development Project (UCDP) is committed to protecting the privacy, dignity, and rights of all individuals — especially the children and families we serve. This page explains how we handle personal data and photographs in our work.

📜 Data Protection Certificate

UCDP holds a Data Protection Certificate issued in accordance with Kenyan law. We are registered and compliant with the requirements of the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

Use of Photographs

UCDP uses photographs of the children, families, and communities we work with for the following purposes:

  • Documenting our programme activities and impact
  • Reports to donors, partners, and the public
  • This website and official UCDP communications
  • Social media and awareness campaigns

All photographs are taken with the informed consent of the individuals concerned, or their parents/guardians where the subject is a minor. Consent is obtained verbally and/or in writing before any photograph is taken or used publicly.

Personal Data We Collect

UCDP may collect personal data from the following groups:

  • Beneficiaries — names, ages, locations, and programme participation records, collected to deliver and monitor our services
  • Donors & Supporters — contact details provided voluntarily through our donation or contact forms
  • Volunteers — application details submitted through our volunteer form
  • Website visitors — basic usage data collected through standard web hosting logs

How We Protect Your Data

  • Personal data is stored securely and accessed only by authorised UCDP staff
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected
  • Individuals may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data at any time

Cookies & Google Analytics

This website uses two types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — small files that remember your cookie preference. No personal data is stored.
  • Google Analytics cookies — we use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site (e.g. which pages are most visited, approximate country of origin). All IP addresses are anonymised before being processed. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on →

We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing tools. This site complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.

Contact Us About Data

If you have any questions about how UCDP handles your personal data, or to make a data request, please contact us at info@ucdp.org or visit our Contact page.
