Who We Are

A Community Built on Care

Uhuru Community Development Project (UCDP) was founded in 2002 by Davies Okombo — a professional social worker with a vision to protect, support, and empower the most vulnerable children and families in Kisumu, western Kenya.

We work across Kisumu County — in the streets, schools, clinics, and communities — running programmes that address poverty, homelessness, lack of education, and social exclusion. Everything we do is rooted in respect for human dignity and a belief that every child deserves a future.

"Uhuru" — meaning freedom in Swahili — reflects our core belief that every child deserves to live and grow in freedom and dignity, regardless of their circumstances.

UCDP is an officially registered Community Based Organisation (CBO), politically and denominationally independent, operating in partnership with Uhuru e.V. — our long-standing support organisation based in Germany.
