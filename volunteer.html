Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to volunteer? +

No prior experience is required for most roles. What matters most is a genuine commitment to our mission, a respectful attitude towards the community, and a willingness to learn. For clinical roles, relevant qualifications are required.

How long do I need to commit? +

The minimum recommended placement is two weeks, as shorter periods can be disruptive for the children and communities we serve. Longer placements of 1–6 months are encouraged and have a much greater impact.

Is accommodation provided? +

UCDP does not currently provide accommodation directly, but we are happy to advise on affordable and safe housing options in Kisumu for international and out-of-town volunteers. Please ask us when you get in touch.

Is there a fee to volunteer? +

UCDP does not charge a placement fee. Volunteers are responsible for their own travel, accommodation, and living costs. Any direct contribution to our programmes is welcome but entirely optional.

Can international volunteers apply? +

Yes — we warmly welcome international volunteers. You will need a valid volunteer or tourist visa for Kenya. We can provide a placement letter to support your visa application if needed.

Will I receive a reference or certificate? +

Yes. All volunteers who complete their placement receive an official UCDP certificate of service. We are also happy to provide a reference letter upon request after completion.
