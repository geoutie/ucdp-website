About Uhuru Community
Development Project

Over two decades of walking alongside the most vulnerable children and families in Kisumu, West Kenya.

Our Story

Born from a Vision for Children

The Uhuru Community Development Project (UCDP) was founded in 2002 by Davies Okombo, a professional and trained Kenyan social worker, with a clear and urgent mission: to support destitute, hopeless, homeless, and defenseless children in Kisumu, West Kenya.

The name "Uhuru" — meaning freedom in Swahili — reflects the core belief that every child deserves to live and grow in freedom and dignity, regardless of their circumstances.

UCDP is a politically and denominationally independent non-profit organisation, officially registered with Kenya's Ministry of Gender, Sports, Culture and Social Services as a Community Based Organisation (CBO). Our board consists of 5 permanent members, and practical implementation is led by our founder, Davies Okombo.

Our Journey

Milestones Over the Years

2002

UCDP Founded

Davies Okombo establishes the organisation in Manyatta, Kisumu, to support street children and vulnerable families.

2004

Internship Programme Launched

UCDP begins hosting student interns from Germany, building international partnerships and enriching programme delivery.

2005

Partnership with Uhuru e.V. (Germany)

The German support association "Uhuru e.V. – Gemeinsam für Kinder in Kenia" is founded in Tübingen, providing crucial international backing.

2010+

Expansion of Programmes

Launch of the Kindergarten, Safe House, Community Clinic, and Skills Training programmes, reaching thousands more beneficiaries.

Today

20+ Years of Impact

UCDP continues to serve the Kisumu community through 8 active projects, touching thousands of lives across Manyatta and beyond.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

🤝

Community-First

We work alongside communities, not above them. Every decision is grounded in the real needs of the people we serve.

🛡️

Child Protection

The safety, dignity, and wellbeing of every child is non-negotiable — it sits at the heart of everything we do.

🌱

Sustainability

We focus on long-term, lasting change through education, skills, and community empowerment.

💪

Empowerment

We believe in equipping individuals with tools and knowledge to shape their own futures with confidence.

🌍

Partnership

We collaborate openly with local volunteers, international partners, and community members to multiply impact.

⚖️

Independence

We are politically and denominationally independent — serving all people in our community without discrimination.
